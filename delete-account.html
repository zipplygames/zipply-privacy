How to Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your account from ZIPPLY, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the ZIPPLY app and go to the Home Page.
  2. Tap on the Profile Icon located at the top right corner.
  3. On the Profile Page, scroll down and tap the Delete Account button.
  4. Confirm your action. Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted from our system.
